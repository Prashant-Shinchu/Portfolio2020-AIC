Aim of our project

The main idea behind the app is to connect people together with emotional bond of music and it's purity. In india we have already given unnecessary priorities to many foriegn's social media & online entertainment plateforms where we are wasting our time and providing our private data to foriegn tech gaints.

We can achive our goal by combining both social media and entertainment service together. So here is the main concept where you can enjoy listening your favorite piece of music & podcast also you can make friends around the world having the same taste in music as you have with almost similar emotional connection.

The app we are going to make is basically a combination of whatsApp and Spotify. This app has an heart beat monitoring sensor and it output the heart beat rate when a user listens to his/her favorite music/song. User's music feed and heart beat rate will be stored in a secure database and it will be recognized by the machine learning algorithm to recommend the best music/podcast and suggest friends having similar taste in music/podcast.

User's can chat online, share their music feed to their friends, they can listen same music in pair or in a group at the same time with the new pairing feature.

Features

All the contents will be relevant to the user such as some of the most underrated music and podcasts will be provided in this app. Many of us have great music & information taste and we want someone or a group to join us and have fun, entertainment together so this would be better approach to join social media and music streaming service together.

Other features such as music feed section, playlist, saved files, chat window, profile section, friends list, account details, heart beat monitoring window and share features, pairing with you friends feature, send and accept friend request feature and many more.

Special

This concept of merging both social media and music/podcast streaming service together would be the next gamechanging revolution. this really excites us when we think of listening our favorite music and podcast together with our friends around the world even if they are strangers but we are all humans. Music has no hate and violence like other social media plateforms where music will help to make peace among people. Other exciting features will be revealed when the app will be ready to use. Now we can have our own social media app with both entertainment(Music) and learning(Podcast).
